Flu Vaccine

Brand: FluShield

Disease Targeted: Influenza

Dosage Profile: One dose annually

Description: FluShield is an annual vaccine that protects against the most common strains of influenza. It is particularly recommended for the elderly and those with weakened immune systems.

COVID-19 Vaccine

Brand: CoviGuard

Disease Targeted: COVID-19

Dosage Profile: Two doses, three weeks apart

Description: CoviGuard provides robust protection against COVID-19, including variants. It is highly recommended for all age groups, especially those at higher risk of severe illness.

Shingles Vaccine

Brand: Shingrix

Disease Targeted: Herpes Zoster (Shingles)

Dosage Profile: Two doses, two to six months apart

Description: Shingrix is a highly effective vaccine to prevent shingles and its complications. It is recommended for adults over 50 years of age.
